Board Programming Guide

Kindly follow the steps below to program the EMS board and configure for deployment and integration.

  1. 1. Connect the Power supply to the EMS board (via the 5v power supply unit or a start up cable)
  2. 2. After the EMS device starts up and the power LED turns red, connect the USB programming cable to the device and your computer
  3. 3. Ensure that your computer is connected to a reliable internet connection
  4. 4. Launch the EMS-Flasher.exe application
  5. 5. Select the SIM card configuration, if required.
  6. 6. Enter the Customer ID i.e. 416813 that has been provided to you, and press the Send to Customer ID button.
  7. 7. Monitor the progress and logs on the application, the process usually takes 90 seconds - 2 minutes
  8. 8. A notification dialog is shown on successful, completion
  9. 9. Disconnect the USB cable from the EMS device
  10. 10. Wait for about 5 seconds, and press the reset button
